Burger

A burger is a patty of ground meat grilled and placed between two halves of a bun. Slices of raw onion, lettuce, bacon, mayonnaise, and other ingredients add flavor. Burgers are considered an American food but are popular around the world.

Biriyani

Biryani is a fragrant, layered rice dish popular in South Asia, particularly in India, Pakistan, and Bangladesh. It's made with rice (often basmati), meat (like chicken, lamb, or goat), and a blend of aromatic spices. Vegetables can also be used in vegetarian versions. The dish is cooked with a slow, gentle technique, often involving steaming or "dum" cooking, which helps to develop the flavors.

Kedgeree/Khichuri

Kedgeree (or occasionally kitcherie, kitchari, kidgeree, kedgaree, kitchiri, khichuri, or kaedjere) is a dish consisting of cooked, flaked fish (traditionally smoked haddock), boiled rice, parsley, hard-boiled eggs, curry powder, lemon juice, salt, butter or cream, and occasionally sultanas.
