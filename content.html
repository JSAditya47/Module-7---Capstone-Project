Pizza

Pizza is a beloved, versatile dish originating from Naples, Italy, known for its delicious combination of flavors and customizable toppings. Typically, pizza consists of a flat, crusty base of wheat-based dough, topped with a tomato-based sauce, mozzarella cheese, and a variety of other ingredients like vegetables, meats, and herbs.

Chicken Curry

Chicken curry from the Indian subcontinent typically features chicken stewed in a tomato-based sauce seasoned with aromatic spices. This recipe, like many others, calls for curry powder (a spice blend made with coriander, turmeric, cumin, and chili powder).
